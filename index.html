Inspestor (Mobile Application)

   

Awarded as the Best Thesis in Information Technology for the Batch 2023 graduatess, our project was an outstanding requirement for my undergraduate degree programme. My teammates and I proudly developed an offline app that can detect insect pests in rice fields using mobile phones. I took on the role of UI designer and front-end developer, using Android Studio to power our detection model and using the SSD-MobileNet framework for deep learning training. Again, this impressive application is the result of a collaborative effort with the Department of Agriculture and local government agencies.
